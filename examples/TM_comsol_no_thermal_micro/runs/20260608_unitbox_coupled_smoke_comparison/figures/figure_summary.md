# Figure Summary

## none_m12/final_alpha_none_m12.png

Plots final nodal alpha for the raw-coordinate controlled run.

Visual takeaway: alpha is nearly uniform across the domain, consistent with
`alpha_mean=0.1677`, `alpha_std=0.0021`, and bulk/notch drive ratio near 1.

Conclusion support: diagnostic observation only; it supports the claim that
this controlled run stayed on a uniform/background branch.

## none_m12/final_He_current_none_m12.png

Plots final current drive for the raw-coordinate controlled run.

Visual takeaway: the current drive is broad, with bulk/notch He ratio about
1.005 and bottom/notch ratio about 0.963.

Conclusion support: diagnostic observation only.

## unitbox_m12/final_alpha_unitbox_m12.png

Plots final nodal alpha for the unit-box controlled run.

Visual takeaway: alpha localizes around the notch region, with alpha_max about
1.0005 and alpha_mean about 0.104.

Conclusion support: diagnostic observation only; it supports the branch change
from uniform/background to notch-localized in this controlled run.

Visual takeaway: the drive is notch amplified, with bulk/notch He ratio about
0.000344 and bottom/notch ratio about 0.000279.

Conclusion support: diagnostic observation only.

## stress/reaction figures

The stress-strain and reaction-strain figures show the two controlled branches
have different reaction paths. These figures should not be treated as physical
validation; they flag reaction behavior as a quantity to monitor in longer
runs.
